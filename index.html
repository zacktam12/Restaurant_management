Restaurant Management Web Service

A REST-based restaurant management system developed as part of a Tourism Management Platform. Enables restaurant listing, reservations, and service integration with tour, hotel, and taxi systems.

Core Features

Restaurant Listings

Manage restaurant profiles including location, cuisine type, opening hours, and service availability.

Table Reservations

Allow customers to reserve tables through the Tourism Management Platform using REST services.

Menu Management

Create and update menus with categories, pricing, and availability status.

System Workflow

1

Register & Authenticate

Restaurant managers create accounts and log in securely.

2

Manage Services

Add restaurant details, menus, and available seating.

3

Consume & Provide Services

Exchange data with tour, hotel, and taxi services via REST APIs.
